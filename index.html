About Us.

A's Bakehouse was founded in 2001 by Agnes Beatriz, a passionate baker with a love for creating delicious treats. Inspired by coffe break times, Agnes started baking out of their home kitchen, sharing their creations with friends and family.

The positive response and encouragement from loved ones led Agnes to open a small bakery in Campinas, SP. With a focus on using high-quality ingredients and traditional baking techniques, A's Bakehouse quickly gained a reputation for its homemade goodness.

Over the years, A's Bakehouse has expanded its offerings to include a variety of baked goods, including coffee, banoff and pies. The bakery remains committed to its original values of quality, tradition, and providing a warm and welcoming atmosphere for its customers.
